Our Demands for Justice

1. Immediate Investigation

A thorough, unbiased investigation into the incident involving Kinza Saleem. All responsible authorities must be held accountable.

2. Protection for Victims

Proper protection and support for victims of gender-based violence and harassment in educational institutions.

3. Accountability for Institutions

Educational institutions must establish transparent processes to handle harassment complaints.

4. Legal Action

Swift legal action against the perpetrators to ensure justice is served without delay.

5. Awareness Programs

Implement mandatory awareness programs about harassment and legal rights for students and staff.
